FAQ

Everything you
need to know

Ask Us Anything →
How long does a typical project take?
+

Most brand and website projects take 6–10 weeks from kickoff to delivery. Complex product design engagements may take 3–6 months. We always provide a detailed timeline before we begin.

What does your process look like?
+

We start with a discovery session to understand your goals, audience, and competitive landscape. Then we move through strategy, design, feedback, and development. You're involved at every stage.

Do you work with startups or only enterprises?
+

We work with both. About half our clients are funded startups who need to establish their brand fast. The other half are established companies investing in a design upgrade. We love both.

What do you need from us to get started?
+

A 50% deposit and a kickoff call. We handle the rest. We're experienced at running projects efficiently and don't require heavy time investments from your team unless you want to be deeply involved.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
+

Yes. Every project includes a post-launch support period, and we offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing design and development support. Many clients work with us for years.
